Ragi Idly

Ragi idly is a nutritious South Indian dish made from fermented ragi (finger millet) batter. Ragi idlies are often served with coconut chutney or sambar for a wholesome and delicious. meal.

Benefits of Ragi

  1. Rich in Nutrients
  2. High in Fiber
  3. Gluten-Free
  4. Low Glycemic Index
  5. Weight Management
  6. Good for Bone Health
  7. Source of Antioxidants
  8. Promotes Digestive Health
  9. Balances Blood Sugar Levels
  10. Versatile and Delicious

Millet dosa

Millet dosa is a popular Indian pancake made from various types of millet flour, such as finger millet (ragi),pearl millet(bajra),or foxtail millet(thinai).

Kichidi

Khichdi is a traditional Indian one-pot dish made by cooking rice and lentils together with a variety of spices and vegetables. It's known for its simplicity,versatility,and comfort,often served as a wholesome meal.

Benefits of Kichidi

  1. Easy to Digest
  2. Balanced Nutrition

JUICES

Carrot Juice

Carrot juice is a great source of vitamins, particularly vitamin A, which is essential for maintaining good vision,skin health,and a strong immune system.It also contains vitamins C,K and various B vitamins.

Beetroot Juice

Beetroot juice is a vibrant and nutrient-rich beverage known for its high levels of antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals, promoting heart health and aiding in detoxification.

Grapes Juice

Grape juice is a flavorful and antioxidant-rich drink known for its potential heart benefits and ability to promote healthy blood pressure.

Apple Juice

Apple juice is a crisp and naturally sweet beverage rich in vitamin C and essential nutrients, supporting overall health and digestion.
